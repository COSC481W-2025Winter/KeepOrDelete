How it Works

  1. Pick a Folder to Review
    Start by selecting any folder on your device: Downloads, Desktop, or a messy project folder. The app will scan and prepare your files for cleanup.
  2. Swipe to Sort Files Your Way
    Review one file at a time using whichever method feels most natural:
    • Drag the file left or right
    • Click the ❌ or ✅ buttons
    • Press ← or → on your keyboard
    You can also rename files with the help of the AI assistant.
  3. Confirm and Clean Up
    When you're finished, finalize your choices. Files marked for deletion are sent to your trash, and the rest stay neatly organized.

Installation Instructions

3. Click "Keep".

4. Click "Show more".

5. Click "Keep anyway".

6. Run the .exe file.
